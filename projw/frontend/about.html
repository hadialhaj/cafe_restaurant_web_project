About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Cafe Cristal, where every cup tells a story. Established in 2010, we've been serving the community with the finest coffee, delicious pastries, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.

Our mission is simple: to create a space where people can connect, relax, and enjoy high-quality beverages and food made with care and passion.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms, ensuring that every sip supports both the environment and the hardworking farmers who grow our coffee.

Fresh Ingredients

We use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients in all our offerings.

Made with Love

Every item on our menu is prepared with care and attention to detail.

Community Focused

We're committed to supporting our local community and initiatives.

Award Winning

Recognized as the best local cafe for three consecutive years.

Our Journey

Start

Cafe Cristal opened its doors with a simple mission: serve great coffee in a welcoming environment.

2013

Expanded our menu to include homemade pastries and light lunch options, becoming a neighborhood favorite.

2016

Started our direct trade program, sourcing beans directly from sustainable farms in Latin America.

2019

Won "Best Local Cafe" award for the first time, recognizing our commitment to quality and community.

2022

Launched our online ordering system, making it easier for customers to enjoy our offerings from home.

2023

Expanded our community programs, hosting weekly coffee tasting events and barista workshops.
